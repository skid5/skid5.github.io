iPhone 13 to iPhone 17

11/22/2025

After 3 generations of iPhones have passed, the iPhone 17 seemed to be the first phone that was worth the upgrade. It was about time that Apple made something worth upgrading to.

I've been using the new iPhone 17 base model for about 2 weeks now, and I can say the 120Hz is probably the best part about it. Coming from a base model iPhone 13, the experience is already great. The iPhone 17 could've been a "filler" phone that they put out every year if it wasn't for the decision to put a Pro display on a base model.

The iPhone 17 is the first model in a while that, in my opinion, provides a significant upgrade (the last one being the iPhone X). The cherry on top is the 120Hz screen. I remember my first upgrade from a 75Hz monitor to a 165Hz one. I feel the same excitement with my phone. I was never a photography person, so I didn't see the worth of upgrading to a Pro or Pro Max model just for the screen.

The camera control is also another unexpected feature for me. I had always thought it was kind of lame for Apple to add an ugly dent that you can feel (with a case) on the side of your phone. After playing around with it, I found that it would be of the most use to me if I used it for zooming. Also, center stage has been an interesting experience for me. I feel like I still need to get used to how it works. It could just be me, but it is a lot harder than it looks on the reveal.

Overall, I am very satisfied with the upgrade. It's been a while since I've been able to enjoy a long-lasting battery, and I hope to keep the battery that way with an 80% charge limit. It seems that everyone else is also making the upgrade, seeing that I had a hard time finding them in stock at any Apple stores near me.
